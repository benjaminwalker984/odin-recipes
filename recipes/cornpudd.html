Grandma's Corn Pudding

Description

This easy corn pudding is a little sweet, a little savory, and totally delicious.

Ingredients

  • 5 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted and slightly cooled
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
  • 2 (14.75 ounce) cans cream-style corn

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F. Grease a 2-quart casserole dish.
  2. Whisk eggs in a large bowl. Add milk, melted butter, sugar, and cornstarch; whisk until well combined.
  3. Stir in drained corn and cream-style corn until fully blended.
  4. Pour mixture into the prepared casserole dish
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown on top, about 1 hour.
