Mac & Cheese

Description

This Mac & Cheese was submitted By Judy on Allrecipes and is sure to please. Topped with buttered bread crumbs and made with a sauce from scratch to coat the noodley goodness.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni Noodles: 8 ounces uncooked elbow macaroni
  • Butter: 1/4 cup salted butter
  • Flour: 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • Milk: 2 1/2 cupts milk, or more as needed
  • Cheese: 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar, 1/2 cup finely grated Parmesan cheese
  • Seasonings: salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • Bread crumbs
    • 2 tablespoons salted butter
    • 1/2 cup dry bread crumbs
    • 1 pinch ground paprika

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease an 8-inch square baking dish
  2. To make the macaroni: bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add macaroni and simmer, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, approximately 8 minutes. Drain and transfer to the prepared baking dish.
  3. While the macaroni is cooking, melt 1/4 cup butter in a medium skillet over low heat. Whisk in flour and stir until the mixture becomes paste-like and light golden brown, 3 to 5 minutes.
  4. Gradually whisk 2 1/2 cups milk into the flour mixture, and bring to a simmer
  5. Stir in shredded Cheddar and finely grated Parmesan cheeses; season with salt and pepper. Cook and stir over low heat until cheese is melted and sauce has thickened, 3 to 5 minutes, adding up to 1/2 cup more milk if needed
  6. Pour cheese sauce over macaroni and stir until well combined.
  7. Make Bread crumb topping: melt 2 tablespoons butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add bread crumbs; cook and stir until well coated and browned.
  8. Spread bread crumbs over macaroni and cheese, then sprinkle with paprika.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven until topping is golden brown and macaroni and cheese is bubbling, about 30 minutes.
